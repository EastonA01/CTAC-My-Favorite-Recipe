Key Lime Pie

Key lime pie is my favorite food because despite its simplicity in its creation- key lime pie has a refreshing level of tartness that I find absolutely irresistible. Despite the allure of some other tasty treats such as peanut butter pie- key lime has it beat due to its ability to be enjoyed endlessly. With a combination of sweetness, tartness and citrus- it truly is a dessert that can be enjoyed by anyone even in the hottest of days!

Make sure to try it with a graham cracker crust for the ultimate experience!

You'll be hooked from the first bite!
